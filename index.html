About FR3E

FR3E is an entity to take legal action against unproportionate, exclusionary, and discriminatory AML/CFT laws in the EU, aiming at their annulment or strict revision in accordance with international human rights law. We believe that the EU's laws to enact Anti-Money Laundering and Countering the Financing of Terrorism (AML/CFT) are illegal and violate fundamental rights enshrined in the UN Convention on Human Rights (UNCHR) and the European Convention on Human Rights (ECHR).

Learn More

Recent Actions

FR3E is currently working to file for an annulment of the EU's new AML package consisting of AML6, AMLA and AMLD. The EU's new AML package establishes a new inter-european financial intelligence agency, limits the transaction of cash and significantly increases the surveillance in cryptocurrencies without due cause.
